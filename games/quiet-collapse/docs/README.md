# The Quiet Collapse

Keyboard-first cosmic-horror investigation game built on Playloom.

## Premise
You are alone aboard the `Eidolon`, chasing evidence of a universe-scale collapse from the border systems to the deep void of Talassa. You cannot stop the mechanism. You can only decide what to do with the truth before the Silence closes around it.

## Controls
1. `Left / Right` or `A / D`
   - Title: select menu option
   - Route: choose destination
   - Scan: switch active band
   - Doctrine: choose packet policy
2. `Up / Down` or `W / S`
   - Scan: tune the active band
3. `Enter`
   - Confirm selection
   - Resolve a scan early
4. `Space`
   - Attempt to lock the active scan band
5. `Esc`
   - Return to title from route selection
6. `H`
   - Toggle help card
7. `M`
   - Toggle audio
8. `C`
   - Continue saved run from title
9. `N`
   - Start a fresh run from title

## Gameplay Loop
1. Start or continue a run from the title screen.
2. Choose one destination for the current chapter.
3. Spend fuel to reach the site and stabilize three scan bands under drift pressure.
4. Recover a clean, partial, or corrupted fragment based on scan quality.
5. Decide whether to `Broadcast`, `Archive`, or `Suppress` the packet.
6. Survive until Talassa or fail from fuel loss, hull collapse, focus collapse, or too much trace.

## Systems
1. `Fuel`: travel budget.
2. `Hull`: physical ship integrity.
3. `Focus`: mental clarity under cosmic pressure.
4. `Trace`: how visible you are to the Silence.
5. `Truth`: total packets recovered across the route.

## Scope
1. Five chapters, including a final Talassa approach.
2. Multiple endings based on truth recovered, doctrine choices, and failure state.
3. Save/load at chapter boundaries through local storage.
